About ICLET 2025

The International Conference on Libraries and Emerging Technologies for Smart Knowledge Ecosystems (ICLET 2025) explores the profound impact of new technologies on the information landscape. These advancements are reshaping the way information is developed, processed, preserved, and disseminated, transforming entire ecosystems. Libraries—public, academic, and special—are adapting through significant reengineering to meet the dynamic needs of users in a rapidly evolving digital age.

ICLET 2025 provides a platform for experts, practitioners, and educators to engage in meaningful discussions about the future of libraries. We invite technologists, implementers, and trainers to join us in shaping the future of information systems.

Why Join Us

The International Conference on Libraries & Emerging Technologies (ICLET 2025) offers a unique platform for academics, researchers, professionals, and technology innovators to:

  • Engage with Global Experts
  • Explore Cutting-edge Innovations
  • Network and Collaborate
  • Enhance Professional Skills
  • Contribute to Sustainable Futures

Whether you’re a librarian, technologist, policymaker, or student — ICLET 2025 is your opportunity to be part of a global movement redefining knowledge ecosystems.

How To Get Here

Poornima College of Engineering is located in Jaipur city of Rajasthan in Northern India, around 270 km south of Delhi. The following guidelines may help you to reach the conference venue.

By Plane

Delhi International Airport is closest if a direct flight to Jaipur is available. Poornima College Engineering is approximately 13 Km from Sanganer Airport, Jaipur.

By Train

Indian railways connect Jaipur to almost all major cities of the country. There are three railway stations other than the main railway station of Indian railways in Jaipur city: The PCE campus is located 22 km from the central railway station (Jaipur Junction). This campus is 14 Km from Durgapura Railway Station, 16 Km from Gandhinagar Railway Station, and 10 Km from Jagatpura Railway Station.

By Road

From National Highway 8:
Connects Jaipur to Delhi, Ajmer, Udaipur, Ahmadabad, Vadodara, Mumbai
From National Highway 11:
Connects Jaipur to Agra → Jaipur → Bikaner
From National Highway 12:
Connects Jabalpur → Bhopal → Khilchipur → Aklera → Jhalawar → Kota → Bundi → Devli → Tonk → Jaipur

Following any route, one can reach the Sindhi Camp Bus Stand (the Central Bus Stand of Jaipur city). From Sindhi camp, which is 22 km away from Poornima College Engineering campus, one can take a bus, prepaid taxi, Ola/Uber/Taxi, or an auto to reach the PCE campus.
